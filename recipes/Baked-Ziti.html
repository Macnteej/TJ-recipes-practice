Baked ziti

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 16oz. box of ziti (or whatever pasta noodle you prefer)
  • 28 ounces of pasta sauce
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 8 oz of mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup Ricotta cheese

Preparation

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil. Add salt and olive oil to your liking
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  3. Brown ground beef in a medium sauce pan. add seasoning of your choice
  4. Once ground beef is cooked, drain beef and add pasta sauce to beef. Simmer until pasta is ready.
  5. Once pasta is cooked, strain and add pasta sauce to the pasta to coat.
  6. Grease a 13"by 9" pan and add half the pasta, then half the sauce. Layer the Ricotta and mozzarella. Repeat with rest of ingredients.
  7. Place dish into oven for 25-30 minutes until top layer of cheese is melted and golden.
  8. Allow to rest for 2-3 minutes after finished baking.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
