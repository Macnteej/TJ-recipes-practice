Breakfast Smoothie

Description

Ingredients

  • 8 oz milk
  • 1 scoop of vanilla protein powder or flavor of choice
  • Frozen fruit and veggies of your choice

Prep

  1. Begin by adding your milk to your blender of choice
  2. Add your scoop of protein to the milk
  3. Add as much frozen fruit or vegetables as you'd like
  4. Blend until the consistency you like
